Who is Abo Zaid ?

Early Life & Education

Ahmed Abouzaid was born and raised in Tanta, Egypt. From a young age, he was passionate about learning and technology. He pursued his studies at the Faculty of Engineering, Tanta University, where he developed strong analytical skills. However, he always felt drawn to teaching and sharing knowledge.

Career Shift & Challenges

Despite working as an engineer, Ahmed realized his true passion was content creation. In 2012, he launched Droos Online, an educational YouTube channel. His journey wasn’t easy—he faced criticism from family and society, who doubted his decision to leave engineering. However, he remained committed, proving his choice was right.

The Best Quote he loves is:

"If you want to accomplish something you've never done before, you have to do something you've never done before. Sometimes that means committing yourself before you feel ready."
